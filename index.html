How to play

Four simple steps. No writing. No pressure.

01
Set the space
Phone face down. Read the grounding card. Give yourself 15 minutes.
02
Find your trigger
Pick the card that matches what set you off — something in your head, something you did or didn't do, or something someone else did.
03
Name the feeling
Find your Big Emotion card. Place a sticker to track it. No writing — the sticker does the work.
04
Move through it
Draw an In the Moment card for right now. Then a Dig Deeper card when you're ready to go further.
